TLS Handshake Explanation:

ClientHello - lists supported ciphers;

ServerHello - selects cipher suite and sends certificate;

Key exchange - session key derived;

Finished - encrypted communication starts.



MITM Protection:

Certificates signed by trusted CAs;

Public-key validation prevents spoofing;

Session keys unique per connection.



TLS in Websites:

Ensures confidentiality, integrity and authenticity;

Protects login forms, payments and any data transfer.
